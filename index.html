Preparations Before Vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the strenght of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19 vaccine work :

Avoid alcoholic beverage

Excessive alcohol consumption can weaken our immune system, making the body an easier target for disease. This is because the defense cells are affected by excessive alcohol consumption.

Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on the immune system. Prolonged stress can increase cortisol production and affect our health.

Eat healty food

A balanced diet allows the body to obtain the necessary nutrients for our immune system to protect us.

Get enough sleep

During sleep, immunological memory increases, this refers to the body's ability to defend itself against future infections.

Exercise or physical activity

Exercise causes changes in antibodies and white blood cells (WBC). White blood cells are the cells of the immune system that fight disease. These antibodies or white blood cells circulate faster, so they can detect diseases faster than before.
